1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve functionality, and analyze user behavior. Cookies may be classified as:
•	Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
•	Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.
•	First-Party Cookies: Set by Firecourt Corp.
•	Third-Party Cookies: Set by partners and service providers.
________________________________________

•	Performance and Analytics: Collect data on how users interact with our website to improve functionality and user experience.
•	Personalization: Tailor content, ads, and recommendations based on your preferences.
•	Marketing: Track user interactions with our ads and measure campaign effectiveness.

5. Managing Cookies
You can control your cookie preferences through the following methods:
Browser Settings
Most browsers allow you to:
•	Block or delete cookies.
•	Set preferences for first-party and third-party cookies.
Refer to your browser’s help documentation for guidance.
Cookie Management Tool
We provide a cookie banner when you first visit our site, enabling you to:
•	Accept all cookies.
•	Customize your cookie preferences.
Opt-Out Options
•	For Google Analytics: Use the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
•	For personalized ads: Visit the Network Advertising Initiative’s opt-out page.
