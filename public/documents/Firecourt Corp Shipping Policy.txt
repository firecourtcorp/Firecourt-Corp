Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this to monitor your shipment’s progress.
________________________________________
6. International Shipping
•	Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country’s authorities.
•	Delivery times for international orders may be extended due to customs clearance.
________________________________________
7. Damaged, Lost, or Delayed Shipments
Damaged Shipments
•	If your order arrives damaged, please contact us at firecourt.corp.official@gmail.com within 48 hours of delivery. Include photos of the damage and the packaging.
•	We will arrange for a replacement or refund as applicable.
Lost Shipments
•	If your tracking information indicates delivery but you haven’t received the package, contact us immediately.
•	We will work with the carrier to investigate and resolve the issue.
Delayed Shipments
•	While we strive to meet delivery timelines, delays may occur due to weather, customs, or unforeseen circumstances. We appreciate your patience in such cases.
________________________________________
8. Changes to Shipping Information
If you need to update your shipping address after placing an order:
•	Contact us within 24 hours of placing the order.
•	Once shipped, address changes cannot be guaranteed, and additional fees may apply.
