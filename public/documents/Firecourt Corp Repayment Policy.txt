Firecourt Corp Refund and Repayment Policy
Effective Date: November 20, 2024
At Firecourt Corp, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction. This Refund and Repayment Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds or repayments are provided for our products and services.
________________________________________
1. General Refund Policy
Refunds are subject to the following conditions:
Eligibility for Refund
•	Refunds are available for defective or non-functional products, errors in billing, or other qualifying circumstances outlined in this policy.
•	Requests must be made within 30 days of purchase unless otherwise specified.
•	Proof of purchase (e.g., receipt, invoice, or transaction ID) is required for all refund claims.
Non-Refundable Items
The following are generally non-refundable:
•	Opened or used physical products (e.g., liquor bottles, supplements).
•	Digital subscriptions or downloads after access or activation.
•	Customized or personalized items unless defective.
________________________________________
2. Audio Tech Products
•	Subscription Services: 
o	Refunds for DAW (Cubes) subscriptions are only provided if cancellation occurs within 7 days of initial activation.
o	No refunds are issued for partial subscription periods or unused time.
•	VSTs, MIDI Controllers, and Digital Goods: 
o	Refunds are available if the product has not been downloaded or activated.
________________________________________
3. Liquor Products
•	Refunds or replacements are available for: 
o	Damaged products upon delivery.
o	Incorrect orders or missing items.
•	Claims must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence.
•	Refunds are not issued for taste preferences or opened bottles.
________________________________________
4. Fitness and Med Products
•	Refunds for fitness equipment or supplements are available for: 
o	Defective or damaged items.
o	Unused items in their original packaging within 14 days of delivery.
•	No refunds are issued for used or opened biochips, supplements, or consumables unless defective.
________________________________________

•	Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10 business days.
•	If refunds cannot be processed to the original method, an alternative arrangement (e.g., store credit) will be provided.
________________________________________
7. Repayments for Errors
In cases where you are overcharged or billed incorrectly, we will:
•	Investigate and verify the claim.
•	Issue a repayment for the overcharged amount within 10 business days of claim approval.
